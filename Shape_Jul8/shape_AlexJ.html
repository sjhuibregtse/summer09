This is my hour glass. It goes from 60% to 10% then 10% to 60%. That's how it looks like and hour glass. I came up with the idea by trying to make a house but instead I made a hour glass which I like better. The hour glass is what I use at friends house to time brushing my teeth.
Hurry! The hour glass is running out of time!
The second shape I made is simular to the hour glass. It is a bee hive. The bee hive is 10% to 25% (including 15%). And then 25% to 10%. Bee's fly in and out to collect nector.
Let's make sure that we don't mistake this bee hive for a pinata!
